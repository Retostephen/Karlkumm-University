Admission Requirements

Review the requirements for your desired program level

Undergraduate Requirements

  • WAEC/NECO certificate with 5 credits including English & Math
  • JAMB UTME score (minimum 180)
  • Post-UTME screening test
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • State of origin certificate
  • Passport photographs

Graduate Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree from recognized university
  • Minimum CGPA of 2.50 (or equivalent)
  • Academic transcripts
  • Two academic references
  • Research proposal (for PhD applicants)
  • English proficiency test (for international students)

International Students

  • Equivalent of Nigerian secondary school certificate
  • TOEFL/IELTS scores for English proficiency
  • Valid passport and visa
  • Medical certificate
  • Financial proof/sponsorship letter
  • Police clearance certificate

Direct Entry

  • A-Level, IJMB, or equivalent qualification
  • Minimum of two A-Level passes
  • WAEC/NECO certificate
  • JAMB Direct Entry form
  • University screening test
  • All required documentation
