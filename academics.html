Academic Requirements

Admission requirements by program level

Undergraduate Programs

  • WAEC/NECO with 5 credits including English and Mathematics
  • JAMB UTME score (minimum varies by program)
  • Post-UTME screening examination
  • Age requirement: 16 years minimum
  • Medical fitness certificate

Graduate Programs (Masters)

  • Bachelor's degree with minimum CGPA of 2.50
  • Academic transcripts from previous institutions
  • Two academic recommendation letters
  • Statement of purpose
  • English proficiency test (for international students)

PhD Programs

  • Master's degree with minimum CGPA of 3.50
  • Research proposal in area of specialization
  • Three academic recommendation letters
  • Publications in reputable journals (preferred)
  • Research methodology knowledge

Professional Programs

  • Relevant work experience (varies by program)
  • Professional certifications (if applicable)
  • Employer recommendation (for sponsored candidates)
  • Portfolio of work (for creative programs)
  • Interview and assessment tests
